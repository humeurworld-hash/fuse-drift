# Privacy Policy — Fuse: Mourk Run

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

Fuse: Mourk Run ("the App") is a mobile arcade game. This policy explains what data we collect (and don't collect) when you play.

## Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.**

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- No account registration is required
- No name, email, phone number, or any identifier is collected
- No location data is accessed
- No camera or microphone access is requested
- No contacts are accessed
- No advertising IDs or tracking identifiers are collected or used

## Data Stored Locally

All game progress is stored **only on your device** in Godot's standard local save directory. This includes:

- Best scores per level
- Which levels have been unlocked
- Audio volume settings
- Whether the intro and tutorial have been viewed

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. There are no in-app purchases.

## Children

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the App is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we add features in a future update that change how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
