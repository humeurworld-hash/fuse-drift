## Full Description

Fuse is falling through a fractured world — and only you can keep him alive.

**FUSE: MOURK RUN** is a fast-paced vertical dodge game across three increasingly brutal levels. Slide Fuse left and right to avoid hazards, collect glowing Mourk shards to build your streak multiplier, and unleash powerful abilities when things get tight.

**Three Levels. Three Worlds.**
- **Level 1 — The Cave:** Falling rocks. Learn the rhythm.
- **Level 2 — The Verge:** Drones that hunt, scan, and fire. Stay sharp.
- **Level 3 — The Loops:** Time-warping clocks that rewind your position. Survive the chaos.

**Abilities**
- **Ghost Dash** — 2.5 seconds of full invincibility. Use it wisely.
- **Shard Pulse** — Magnetic pull that draws nearby shards toward you.

**Streak System**
Chain Mourk shard pickups to unlock multipliers up to ×4. Break the chain and lose it all.

**Special Shards**
- 🟠 **Orange** — 10-consecutive triggers FUSE STATE: slow hazards and score surge
- 🟣 **Purple** — Slow-time: the world drops to 65% speed for 4 seconds
- 🟡 **Gold** — Jackpot: ×3 multiplier for 8 seconds
- 🟢 **Green** — Heal or gain a shield when already at full health

No ads. No in-app purchases. Just survive.
